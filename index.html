About

Epiphany Music Africa is a gospel worship group based in Kampala, Uganda, passionately committed to glorifying Jesus through music. Founded in 2023, the group began its ministry by leading worship in local churches. In 2024, they expanded their mission by stepping into music production, recording original worship songs that inspire hearts and uplift souls. The vision is to make Jesus famous through Music

Music

Through soul-stirring gospel music, the group makes and leads worship experiences that ignite faith and celebrate the presence of God

Charity

The group actively engages in charitable outreach, serving communities in need with love and generosity.

Music Workshops

Workshop sessions mentor both vocalists and instrumentalists, equipping them with technical skills, spiritual depth, and leadership insight..
